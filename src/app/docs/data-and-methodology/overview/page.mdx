## Trusted Data & Harmonized Analysis

Koi delivers auditable analyses by combining trusted lifecycle GHG data, industry analysis, and market intelligence from leading sources worldwide. Our harmonized approach applies a consistent methodology across all models, enabling fair comparisons and comprehensive investigations into each solution's climate impact potential.

**Comprehensive Data Integration**: We start with data from peer-reviewed LCA studies, industry reports, and authoritative databases to ensure robust impact calculations.

**Software-Assisted Analysis**: Programmatic methodology application enables models that can be built upon, revisited, and scaled into comprehensive portfolio analyses, unlocking advanced analytical capabilities across diverse technologies and markets.

**Transparent Assumptions**: All modeling assumptions and data sources are clearly documented, enabling stakeholders to understand and validate impact projections.

This integrated approach provides investors and decision-makers with the reliable, comparable data needed to evaluate climate solutions and make informed investment and strategic decisions.
