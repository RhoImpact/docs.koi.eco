## How Koi Helps:

**Client Impact Analysis**: Deliver high-quality, scientifically-grounded impact assessments for clients without investing months in custom LCA studies. Koi's standardized methodology ensures consistency and comparability across client engagements.

**Climate Strategy Development**: Help clients identify the highest-impact interventions within their value chains or investment portfolios. Koi's comprehensive climate solution database enables rapid opportunity screening and prioritization.

**ESG Reporting & Ratings Support**: Support clients in meeting disclosure requirements with transparent, auditable avoided emissions data. Koi's clear data provenance and methodology alignment with leading frameworks (WBCSD, Project Frame, PCAF, CDP) ensures credibility.

**Proposal Development**: Quickly generate impact projections and scenario analyses to support client proposals and scoping discussions. Koi enables consultants to showcase potential outcomes before committing to full engagement work.

**Benchmarking**: Compare client climate solutions or portfolios against industry peers using Koi's standardized metrics and extensive climate solution database.
